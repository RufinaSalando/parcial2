About Sergey Lazarev

Sergey Lazarev is one of the most vibrant and emotional artists on the Russian pop scene. His music is a synthesis of electronics, drama, and deep vocals. He has represented Russia at Eurovision, released platinum albums, and drawn inspiration from both Western synthpop and Russian lyricism.

About album

"Happy People Cry Too" is a collection of 12 emotional tracks about love, loss, nostalgia, and acceptance. The album is recorded in a synthwave style with elements of '80s retro pop.

  • 1. Happy people cry too (счастливые тоже плачут)
  • 2. Compasses (Компасы)
  • 3. Native (Родной)
  • 4. Angels don’t die (Ангелы не умирают)
  • 5. I’m with you (Я с тобой)
  • 6. Dalí (Дали)
    7. ITYFE (Я за все тебя благодарю)
